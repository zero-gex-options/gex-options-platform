Premium Spent (5-min) ℹ️ Total Premium Spent (Last 5 Minutes)

Dollar amount spent on SPY options contracts in the most recent 5-minute bucket. Calculated as mid price × volume × 100 (contract multiplier).

Higher values indicate increased options activity and potential institutional interest.

Delta-Weighted Flow ℹ️ Cumulative Delta-Weighted Flow

Running total of directional exposure from options trades. Adjusted by delta to represent equivalent stock exposure.

Positive (Green): Net bullish flow (more calls or aggressive call buying)
Negative (Red): Net bearish flow (more puts or aggressive put buying)
--

Put/Call Notional Value ℹ️ Put vs Call Notional Exposure

Compares the total dollar value of put positions (red) versus call positions (green) in each 5-minute bucket.

Green line above red: More bullish positioning - higher call exposure

Red line above green: More bearish positioning or hedging - higher put exposure

Lines converging: Balanced market sentiment

Notional value = contracts × underlying price × 100, representing the total market exposure.

Premium Spent (5-Minute Buckets) ℹ️ Premium Spent Over Time

Shows the total dollar amount spent on SPY options in each 5-minute period. Each bar represents one 5-minute bucket of aggregated trades.

Higher bars: Increased options activity - often seen during volatile periods or near major support/resistance levels.

Lower bars: Quiet periods with less options trading activity.

Spikes in premium can indicate institutional positioning or hedging activity.

Delta-Weighted Flow ℹ️ Cumulative Delta-Weighted Flow

Running total showing the directional bias of options trades adjusted for delta (equivalent stock exposure).

Rising line: Bullish flow accumulation - more call buying or put selling

Falling line: Bearish flow accumulation - more put buying or call selling

Flat line: Balanced flow - neutral market positioning

This metric helps identify whether smart money is positioning for upside or downside moves.
