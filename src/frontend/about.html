About ZeroGEX™

ZeroGEX™ is a sophisticated options analytics platform designed to provide real-time gamma exposure (GEX) analysis for equity options traders and market participants.

What is Gamma Exposure?

Gamma Exposure (GEX) represents the dollar amount market makers must hedge as the underlying asset moves. When dealers are:

  • Long Gamma (Positive GEX): They buy dips and sell rallies, creating a stabilizing force in the market
  • Short Gamma (Negative GEX): They sell dips and buy rallies, amplifying volatility and price movements

Key Metrics Explained

Net GEX: The difference between call and put gamma exposure. Positive values suggest dealer stabilization, negative values suggest amplification of moves.

Gamma Flip Point: The price level where net GEX crosses zero, marking the transition between positive and negative gamma regimes.

Max Gamma Strike: The strike price with the highest total gamma, acting as a "price magnet" especially near expiration.
